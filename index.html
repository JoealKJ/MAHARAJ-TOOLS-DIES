About Us

Maharaj Tools & Dies had a humble beginning in a small Tool Shop in Mumbai 1992 by Mr. Davis V.P. later shifted to Kerala 1997. The company determined to up hold the core value of Integrity, quality, commitment and innovation under with leadership of Mr. Delvin V. Davis. We are one of the leading Manufacturers of Mold in Agriculture, Plumbing, Electrical, Automobile, Industrial & Household categories in different Polymers like PVC, CPVC, UPVC, PP, ABS, HDPE etc. Our Manufacturing setup includes Injection Molding, Design and Development Centre. Quality lab, Assembly Shop and Die Manufacturing. Trailing of Molds. We believe in total customer satisfaction with our main focus on quality, cost, delivery and services. Our team of experienced engineers with latest software capabilities for delivering best products to the customers. We maintained with highly effcient system & procedures by skilled labour. An expert solution for Plastic and PVC fittings Mold later had a new technology in Exrusion Die area for the Pipe industries & Rain water harvest systems

Now Maharaj Tools & Dies is a leading Manufacturer Of Plastic & Pvc Fitting Mold & Extrusion Dies Leading 25 Years In Manufacturer Of Plastic & Pvc Fitting Injection Molds & Extrusion Dies
